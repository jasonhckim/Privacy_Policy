Find Meal Deals – Support

Need help with the Find Meal Deals iOS app? This page explains how the app works, common issues, and how to contact the developer directly.

i Last updated: November 2025

About the App

Find Meal Deals helps you discover restaurant specials, discounts, and promotions near your current location. You can explore deals, view key details, and navigate to the restaurant with a single tap using your preferred maps app.

How to Use Find Meal Deals

1. Allow Location Access

When you first open the app, iOS will ask for permission to access your location. Granting access allows the app to show deals near you. If you denied this earlier, you can enable it in:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Find Meal Deals

2. Browse Nearby Deals

The home screen lists active deals from restaurants around you. Scroll to explore offers, then tap a deal to see more details such as description, time window, and location.

3. Navigate to a Restaurant

When you find a deal you like, tap the Navigate button. You can open the route in Apple Maps or another supported navigation app installed on your device.

4. Saving Favorites (if available)

If the app version you are using supports favorites, tap the favorite/heart icon on a deal to save it for quick access later in the Favorites section.

Common Questions

No deals are showing near me

  • Confirm location access is set to “While Using the App”.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Some areas may not yet have participating restaurants. Coverage will expand over time.

Location looks incorrect

  • Check that Location Services are enabled for your device.
  • Try closing the app completely and reopening it to refresh your location.

The navigation button doesn’t work

  • Make sure Apple Maps or another maps app is installed and working.
  • If the problem continues, please send us your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happens when you tap the button.

A deal is outdated or incorrect

Restaurant offerings can change. If you see a deal that is no longer valid, please report it using the contact email below and include:

  • Restaurant name
  • Deal title
  • Date and time you visited

Find Meal Deals uses your location only to show nearby deals and help you navigate to restaurants. Location data is not sold to third parties.
